UX/UI

Ux/Ui design is so fucking cool. Being able to go through and prototype something that could potentially make a difference in the way someone lives their life is a whole other level of design. User experience goes so far beyond applications, its designing in virtual, three dimensional space, its advertising, touch screen interfaces, voice control, and so much more. I will always look forward to extending my personal projects beyond the one dimensional design, and try to make something that extends into a usable interface.
